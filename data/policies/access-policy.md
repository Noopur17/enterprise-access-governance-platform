# Enterprise Access & Compliance Policy

## Section 14.4 - Product Management & Marketing Transfers

Employees transitioning into Product Management & Marketing require baseline collaboration access and standard product telemetry access.

If the employee previously belonged to cost center CC-SALES-01, direct write access to customer transactional systems must be removed within 48 hours.

Examples of restricted access:
- Salesforce Opportunity Editing
- CRM Deal Write Access
- Customer Transaction Registry Write Access

Read-only market analysis access may be retained.

## Section 15.1 - Engineering Transfers

Employees moving into Engineering require engineering collaboration access and source code repository access based on manager approval.

## Section 16.2 - Finance Transfers

Employees moving into Finance require finance reporting access. Sales write access should be reviewed and removed when no longer needed.
