# Enterprise Access & Compliance Policy

## Section 14.4 - Product Management & Marketing Transfers

Employees transitioning into Product Management & Marketing require baseline collaboration access.

If the employee previously belonged to cost center CC-SALES-01, direct write access to customer transactional systems must be removed.

Examples:

- Salesforce Opportunity Editing
- CRM Deal Write Access

Read-only Market Analytics access may be retained.

## Section 15.1 - Engineering Transfers

Engineering employees require engineering collaboration access and repository access.

## Section 16.2 - Finance Transfers

Finance employees require finance reporting access.
